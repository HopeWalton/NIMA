Welcome

Welcome to the Mushroom Club of North Idaho!

Founded in 1968 during a fall mushroom class at North Idaho College, the Mushroom Club of North Idaho has been a beacon of mycological exploration and education in the region for over five decades. Our club was born out of a shared passion for mushrooms and a desire to foster a community dedicated to their study and appreciation.

From our humble beginnings meeting in the basement of the courthouse to our current vibrant community, the Mushroom Club of North Idaho has remained committed to its founding goals:

Walks and Forays: We offer regular opportunities for members to explore the natural world and collect mushrooms in their habitats. These excursions not only provide valuable field experience but also serve as a platform for honing scientific methods of mushroom identification.

Scientific Methods of Identification: Through workshops, presentations, and hands-on learning experiences, we empower our members with the knowledge and skills necessary to confidently identify wild mushrooms using scientific methods.

Sharing Wild Mushroom Recipes: Our community celebrates the culinary delights of wild mushrooms by sharing recipes, cooking tips, and culinary adventures. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a novice cook, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Educating the General Public: We are passionate about educating the public about the diverse aspects of wild mushrooms, including their ecological importance, medicinal properties, and cultural significance. Through outreach events, workshops, and publications like our newsletter we aim to inspire curiosity and appreciation for fungi.

Promoting Mushroom Cultivation: Recognizing the potential of mushrooms for sustainable food production and environmental stewardship, we actively promote mushroom cultivation among our members. By sharing resources, techniques, and best practices, we empower individuals to cultivate their own mushrooms at home.

At the Mushroom Club of North Idaho, we are more than just enthusiasts; we are a community united by our love for mushrooms and our dedication to exploring, learning, and sharing our knowledge with others. Whether you're a seasoned forager, a budding mycologist, or simply curious about the world of mushrooms, we welcome you to join us on our mycological journey. Together, let's discover the fascinating world of fungi and cultivate a deeper appreciation for the wonders of nature.
