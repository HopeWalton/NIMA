Who We Are

Nestled in the rugged beauty of North Idaho, our mushroom club is a dynamic hub for conservation, education, and foraging enthusiasts. United by our shared fascination with fungi, we embark on thrilling expeditions into the forest, exploring the diverse ecosystems that house these hidden treasures. Through hands-on learning and community engagement, we're committed to both preserving our local mushroom populations and spreading awareness about their ecological importance. Join us as we delve into the fascinating world of mushrooms, forging connections with nature and each other along the way.

Membership Benefits

Join the Mushroom Club of North Idaho and unlock exclusive benefits tailored for mushroom enthusiasts like you.

With access to Walks and Forays (members only), scientific workshops, culinary adventures, and outreach events, membership offers a unique opportunity to explore, learn, and share your passion for mushrooms with a vibrant community.

Whether you're an experienced forager or a curious beginner, join us on our mycological journey and discover the wonders of fungi together.
